Case Outline: SCP-999's Origin Story and Spread

SCP-999 first appeared on the SCP Foundation Wiki, a collaborative storytelling website established in the late 2000s. The entry describes a benign, playful entity capable of inducing euphoria and laughter in humans and animals alike. Unlike many other SCPs, which are dangerous or inscrutable, SCP-999 is universally non-threatening, creating a striking contrast within the Foundation’s archive.

Theories and Interpretations: Fact, Claim, or Retelling?
